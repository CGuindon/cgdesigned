Student's on Ice Foundation Website

Internationally recognized as a pioneer and leader in Polar Education, SOI offers unique educational expeditions to the Antarctic. We did a re-design of the website that included re-organizing the information architecture, creating re-usable page templates in the CMS and building a new section for alumni stories. My role as the senior designer was to create a new visual direction for the foundation's website and produce all visual deliverables. I also assisted our strategists in research for the information architecture, as well as, defining the user flows and interface.
